OFF THE BEATEN TRACK TOURS

"Off the Beaten Track" Tours are specially-crafted to suit photographers' needs. You are special breed of human being, and we know that you want awesome pictures and those that can blow off your jaws. We will bring you to places that are less touristy and even places that you can't find a single human for days.

EXPERIENCE THE DIFFERENCE

Join us and experience the difference in our tours. We can control Everything except Weather. But, we love all weather! Sunny, rainy, windy, stormy weather, as that is the time to capture the "WOW" moments.

We teach Basic Handling of Equipment, Fundamental Photography - Composition, Depth of Field, Lighting to Advance Photography like Photography Intention and Storyboard Telling, Creative and Abstract Photography. We are in the digital world, so Post Processing Techniques are important too. If we are given availablity of time during the tour, we will conduct a short Adobe Lightroom Workshop. Reviewing of Photos will be done as needed during the trip and before end of the trip. Be amazed that the trip will be acitivites filled and you will not be bored!
